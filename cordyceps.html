CORDYCEPS

The Cordyceps fungus is a central plot element and the cause of the post-apocalyptic world's collapse. The fungus is based on a real-world fungus of the same name, which affects insects in nature.

In the gameshow's world, the Cordyceps fungus has mutated somewhere in South America, in Jakarta, Indonesia, allowing it to infect humans and other mammals. The fungus is highly contagious and spreads through airborne spores, which can infect individuals who inhale them.

The Cordyceps fungus has several stages of infection, each with its unique characteristics and effects on the host. The early stages of infection, such as the spore and runner stages, result in physical transformations, with the fungus taking over the host's body and mind. The later stages, such as the clicker and bloater stages, result in even more severe physical changes, rendering the host almost unrecognizable.

Infected individuals in the gameshow's world are highly aggressive and will attack any uninfected humans they encounter. They are also highly resistant to damage, making them difficult to kill.

In the gameshow's narrative, the Cordyceps fungus has caused a complete collapse of society, with the remaining humans struggling to survive in a world overrun by the infected. The fungus has also forced humanity to adopt new survival tactics, such as scavenging for resources and living in fortified communities.
