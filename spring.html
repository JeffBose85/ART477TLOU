BUS DEPOTTHROUGH THE CITY

Joel and Ellie arrive in Salt Lake City on a mission to find the hospital where the Fireflies are stationed. Ellie is unusually quiet as they make their way. They enter a bus depotruined building where they come across a giraffe, which brings out Ellie's childlike wonder. They watch the giraffes for a bit and Joel brings up concern and reminds that they don't have to do this, they can turn back, Ellie is determined to continue their journey to find the Fireflies, as she says this all can't be for nothing.

As they make their way through a system of underground tunnels with flooded parts and infected, they encounter rapid water that they must cross. While crossing a ruined bus that acts as a bridge, it dislodges from its holding and both Joel and Ellie fall into the rapids. Ellie, who doesn't know how to swim, drowns while Joel manages to save her and bring her to the surface.

As Joel tries to help Ellie recover, a Firefly patrol interrupts them and knocks Joel out.

 Ellie and Joel continue through the town and in the middle of the street a concussion grenade lands at their feet as they are both knocked unconcious

THE FIREFLY LAB

Joel wakes up in a hospital room where Marlene, the leader of the Fireflies, tells him that Ellie is being prepped for surgery to extract a vaccine for Cordyceps. However, Marlene reveals that Ellie will not survive the surgery, but it is necessary to reverse engineer a vaccine. Joel protests, but Marlene has him escorted out of the hospital.

As Joel walks past his gear, he disarms a Firefly soldier and demands to know where the operating room is. He shoots the soldier and rampages through the hospital, killing anyone in his way, until he reaches the operating room. There, Joel is confronted by a doctor who refuses to let him take Ellie. In response, Joel shoots the doctor and escapes with Ellie.

In the parking garage, Marlene tries to reason with Joel, but he shoots and kills her before fleeing in a vehicle. When Ellie wakes up, Joel lies to her, saying that the Fireflies are no longer looking for a cure and that there are many people who are immune.

JACKSON

Joel and Ellie drive back to Jackson, where they plan to stay. However, their truck breaks down just outside of Jackson, so they have to walk the rest of the way. Along the way, Ellie stops Joel and questions him about the events at the hospital, specifically if what he said about the Fireflies is true.

Joel lies to Ellie, telling her that the Fireflies stopped looking for a cure and that there are many others like Ellie who are immune. Ellie seems to accept Joel's explanation, but there are hints of doubt in her expression.
