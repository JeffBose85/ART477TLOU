JOEL MILLER

Joel Miller is the main protagonist of the video game, The Last of Us Part 1, developed by Naughty Dog. HBO Series, The Last of Us. The gameshow takes place in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by infected humans, known as the infected. Joel is a smuggler who lives in a quarantine zone in Boston, Massachusetts, with his partner, Tess.

Joel is initially presented as a cynical and emotionally closed-off survivor, having lost his daughter Sarah during the early stages of the outbreak. However, his life takes a dramatic turn when he is tasked with escorting a young girl named Ellie across the country to a group known as the Fireflies, who believe that Ellie may hold the key to creating a cure for the outbreak.

As Joel and Ellie journey across the country, Joel begins to develop a bond with Ellie, who becomes like a surrogate daughter to him. He begins to open up emotionally and comes to care for her deeply. Throughout the game, Joel is portrayed as a complex and morally ambiguous character, as he is forced to make difficult decisions and engage in violent confrontations in order to protect Ellie.

Joel is depicted as a skilled fighter, with expertise in hand-to-hand combat and firearms. He is also resourceful and able to navigate dangerous environments with ease. He is shown to be a survivor at heart, who is willing to do whatever it takes to protect himself and those he cares about.
