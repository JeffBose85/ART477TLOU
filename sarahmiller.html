SARAH MILLER

Sarah Miller is a side character in the video game The Last of Us HBO TV-Series The Last of Us . She is the daughter of the gameshow's main protagonist, Joel, and is killed in the gameshow's opening scene during the initial outbreak of a fungal pandemic that has decimated the world's population.

Sarah is a young teenage girl who is portrayed as being close to her father. She is shown to be caring, sarcastic, and intelligent, with a strong sense of humor. She also has a rebellious streak, often sneaking out of the house at night to go to parties.

At the start of the gameshow, Joel and Sarah are in Texas and are forced to flee with Tommy as the pandemic spreads. During their escape, Sarah is fatally shot by a soldier and dies in Joel's arms. This event serves as a catalyst for Joel's character development throughout the gameshow, shaping his interactions with other characters and his outlook on life in the post-apocalyptic world.
