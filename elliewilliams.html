ELLIE WILLIAMS

Ellie is one of the main protagonists in the video game "The Last of Us Part 1"HBO TV-Series The Last of Us. She is a 14-year-old girl who is immune to the Cordyceps fungus that has infected most of humanity, leading to the collapse of civilization. She is tough, intelligent, and resourceful, having learned how to survive on her own in the harsh, post-apocalyptic world.

Ellie is initially hesitant to trust Joel, the other protagonist, but eventually forms a strong bond with him as they journey together across the country. She has a sarcastic sense of humor and often makes pop culture references that Joel doesn't understand.

Despite her tough exterior, Ellie has a vulnerable side, and the trauma of losing loved ones weighs heavily on her. She also struggles with the weight of being immune and the pressure that comes with potentially being humanity's last hope for a cure.
