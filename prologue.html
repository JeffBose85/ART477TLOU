HOMETOWN

The prologue takes place in Austin, Texas, in 20132003 a fungal outbreak known as cordyceps has devastated the world. The outbreak originated in South AmericaJakarta, Indonesia and mutated to infect humans, turning them into aggressive and deadly creatures.

The prologue opens with Joel and his daughter, Sarah, trying to celebrate his birthday at home. While watching TV, Sarah falls asleep on the couch, eventually waking up in her own bed. She searches for Joel and eventually finds him fleeing from a runner. Joel kills the runner and Tommy arrives in his truck. eating breakfast before Joel leaves for work. Since it is Joel's birthday Sarah goes into town to repair Joel's watch as a gift. She returns home and eventually Joel aswell. Sarah eventually falls asleep while watching TV with Joel and is waken up by explosions and in her own bed. She looks for Joel to no avail and the neighbor's dog claws at the window. Sarah tries to return the dog when she hears a crash from the neighbor's home and she investigates. She finds the families disabled mother infected and killing the others. Sarah runs out to the street where she is saved from Tommy and Joel as they pull up in a truck. The three attempt to leave town, but are caught in traffic and eventually get into a car accident.

They continue on foot, but encounter the infected creatures, known as runners. They manage to evade them and make it to the outskirts of town, where they are stopped by a U.S. soldier. The soldier is ordered to execute them by his superior, the soldier starts firing but is quickly shot by Tommy. But, it is too late Sarah is hit and dies in Joel's arms.

This traumatic event sets the stage for the rest of the game, as it shapes Joel's character and motivation. He becomes a hardened survivor who is emotionally closed off and has lost faith in humanity. The events of the prologue also introduce the world of The Last of Us, showing the dangers of the infected and the harsh realities of the post-apocalyptic world.
