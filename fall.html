TOMMY'S DAMJACKSON

Joel and Ellie are entering Jackson County, Wyoming. At the hydroelectric dam gate, they are stopped and questioned. However, one of the men recognizes Joel as his brother and lets them in.

Tommy gives them a tour of the dam and shows them the turbines, which are successfully powered up, giving electricity to the town. But their conversation is interrupted by a siren, as the dam is under attack by raiders.

Joel and Tommy fight their way through the raiders to find Maria and Ellie. Tommy sees how much Joel cares for Ellie and realizes that he must take her to the Fireflies. Ellie, however, is not happy with Joel's decision to leave her, so she rides off alone on a horse.

Joel and Tommy chase after her and eventually catch up to her in a farmstead, where they have an argument. Ellie expresses her fear of being with anyone else, and Joel doesn't trust himself to protect her. The argument is cut short as raiders enter the home, but they fight them off.

In the end, Joel makes up his mind that he will continue to travel with Ellie because of how much he cares for her.

In this chapter, Joel and Ellie continue their journey towards Wyoming and come across a couple in the woods who direct them to a nearby settlement. As they pass a hydroelectric dam, they are stopped by a patrol and taken to Jackson, a small town where Tommy, lives. Tommy gives Joel and Ellie a tour of the town, eventually Joel and Tommy go to talk while Maria continues to take Ellie around. Joel asks Tommy to take Ellie to the Fireflies out west. However, Tommy refuses, citing his desire to build a family with Maria.

Joel opens up to him about his doubts about being able to protect Ellie, and he agrees to take her to the Fireflies. However, Ellie is unhappy with the decision and argues with Joel, bringing up the memory of Joel's daughter, Sarah. In the morning Tommy takes Ellie to the stable where they find Joel. Joel had time to think about it and gives Ellie the choice for either Joel or Tommy to take her and Ellie immediately chooses Joel.

THE UNIVERSITY

Joel and Ellie reach the university on horseback, but find it seemingly deserted. They encounter gates that are closed, forcing Joel to look for alternate routes while avoiding infected. When they reach the main campus lab, they find no one around and begin searching for clues. They hear a noise from the upper floors and investigate, discovering leftover monkeys from experiments and more evidence suggesting that the Fireflies have moved to Salt Lake City.

As they try to leave, they are ambushed by a group of looters and have to fight their way out. During the scuffle, Joel is attacked by one of the looters and falls off a balcony, impaling himself on a piece of rebar. they hear and see a group of four men looting around the building. They try to escape but one of them spots them and attacks Joel. The man swings but misses and hits a tree breaking his bat in two. Joel kills the man but realizes the man stabbed him with the broken part of his bat. Ellie helps Joel escape the lab and they flee on their horse, but Joel eventually passes out from blood loss.
