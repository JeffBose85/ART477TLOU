LAKESIDE RESORT

Ellie tries to treat Joel as he tells her to stop and leave him, and to go find Tommy. Ellie refuses and finds supplies to try and stitch Joel up.

Eventually after helping Joel, Ellie travels through the woods and she goes hunting for herself and Joel. She finds a deer and follows it to an abandoned industrial facility, but is interrupted by two hunters, David and James, who offer to trade for the deer. While waiting for James to bring back antibiotics, Ellie and David are attacked by infected and must fight their way out. Eventually the infected are all killed and they create a fire to wait more. While James goes back to get the medicine David and Ellie wait by a fire and talk. David reveals that his group has encountered a crazy man and a little girl, which Ellie realizes is her and Joel. James comes back with a gun raised but David tells him to lower the gun and give Ellie the medicine.

Ellie runs away with the medicine and is eventually tracked by David's group. She evades them for a while but is eventually captured and tries to flee on horseback but the horse is shot and she is captured and taken to David's town, where she is locked up. David attempts to convince Ellie to join his group, but she refuses and is scheduled to be killed in the morning. In the morning however, Ellie reveals that she is infected and kills James before escaping. Ellie runs through the town while evading the searching group through a snowstorm. Eventually she gets into a restaurant-like building where David searches her down in. Ellie escapes out of the room and goes out into the front of the building in a restaurant-like room. David chases her down into the room They have a fight and Ellie ultimately kills David.

Joel wakes up and tries to find Ellie, knocking out some of David's men for information on her whereabouts. He eventually finds her in the restaurant and they both escape the town together.
